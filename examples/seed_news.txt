[BREAKING] A major tech giant announces layoffs of 15%, affecting approximately 12,000 employees

According to informed sources, the globally renowned tech company TechNova announced today that it will conduct large-scale layoffs, with a layoff ratio as high as 15%, expected to affect approximately 12,000 employees.

TechNova CEO Zhang Mingyuan stated in an internal memo: "Facing macroeconomic uncertainty and the rapid transformation of AI technology, we must refocus on our core business and concentrate our resources on artificial intelligence and cloud computing."

Following this announcement, TechNova's stock price rose 3.2% in after-hours trading, with investors welcoming the company's cost-cutting measures. However, on social media, many TechNova employees and former employees expressed dissatisfaction and anxiety.

Industry analyst Li Hua noted: "This is the third wave of large-scale layoffs in the tech industry this year. Companies are shifting labor costs toward AI infrastructure investment, and this trend will continue for the next 12 months."

Union representative Wang Fang stated: "Laying off employees while the company's profits grew 22% last year is unacceptable for the workforce. We will organize collective action to defend employee rights."
